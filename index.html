MADRID SOFÍA HERNÁNDEZ PITARCH 21 YEARS

Hello! My name is Sofía Hernández Pitarch, and I'm a graphic design student from Madrid. My work is based on a strong capacity for adaptability and problem-solving, qualities that allow me to approach each project with an analytical and creative perspective. I enjoy teamwork and always maintain clear and effective communication, ensuring a smooth and collaborative work.

Furthermore, I practice continuous learning, which allows me to update my knowledge and stay current with new design tools, trends, and methodologies. This portfolio brings together the most important projects from my journey as a graphic designer, in which I explain the brief and the design decisions made for each project.

THIS SECTION SHOWCASES MY EDITORIAL DESIGN WORK THROUGH ÁNIMA, AN ART PHOTOGRAPHY MAGAZINE THAT COMBINES POWERFUL AND INTENSE IMAGES WITH EQUALLY STRIKING TYPOGRAPHY. EACH PHOTOGRAPH IS ACCOMPANIED BY A POETIC PIECE THAT ENGAGES IN A DIALOGUE WITH THE IMAGE, CREATING A VISUAL AND EMOTIONAL NARRATIVE THAT REINFORCES ITS MEANING. THE NAME, WHICH MEANS "SOUL" IN ITALIAN, REFLECTS THE INTROSPECTIVE NATURE OF THE PROJECT AND CONNECTS DIRECTLY TO ITS ORIGINS, AS THE MAGAZINE WAS CONCEIVED DURING MY ERASMUS EXCHANGE IN ITALY, INTEGRATING VISUAL LANGUAGE AND PERSONAL EXPLORATION.

PITARK ANIMA PHOTOSHOP / INDESIGN 2025

THIS SECTION SHOWCASES MY EDITORIAL DESIGN WORK THROUGH DARK, A MAGAZINE THAT PRESENTS MY PHOTOGRAPHY PROJECT INSPIRED BY THE UNSETTLING DUALITY EXPLORED IN TWIN PEAKS. RATHER THAN SEEKING RATIONAL ANSWERS, THE LAYOUT IS BUILT UPON SENSATIONS, MIRRORING A UNIVERSE WHERE LOGIC DILUTES AND THE MUNDANE BECOMES UNCANNY. THE DESIGN CENTERS ON DUALITY AS ITS CORE AXIS, EXPLORING A LIMINAL SPACE WHERE LIGHT AND DARKNESS COEXIST AND THE PHYSICAL BLEEDS INTO THE SPIRITUAL. THIS VISUAL NARRATIVE CAPTURES A FRAGMENTED REALITY WHERE GOOD IS TAINTED BY EVIL, AND NOTHING IS EVER COMPLETELY ONE THING OR THE OTHER.

AFLAME

BRANDING

2025

THE PROJECT BRIEF WAS TO DEVELOP A SUB-BRAND FOR A HYPOTHETICAL AFLAME PODCAST, MAINTAINING A CLEAR AND COHERENT RELATIONSHIP WITH THE MAIN BRAND IDENTITY. THE OBJECTIVE WAS TO ADAPT THE BRAND'S VISUAL UNIVERSE TO THE AUDIO FORMAT, CREATING A RECOGNIZABLE, CONTEMPORARY IDENTITY ALIGNED WITH AFLAME'S CREATIVE AND INNOVATIVE VALUES. BASED ON THIS APPROACH, THE DESIGN DECISIONS FOCUSED ON REINTERPRETING THE KEY ELEMENTS OF THE ORIGINAL BRANDING.
